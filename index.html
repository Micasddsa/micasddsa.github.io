Current Projects

Storybook

This this probably my life passion. Storybook is a collection of stories that I've worked on that will be readable for absolutely free and probably is mid.

Project Pearl

Project Pearl is a story about Jason. A young puzzle solver that stumbles across a mystery about his school's Game Development club.

Emerald's Story

Emearld's Story is a book that predates Project Pearl in both development and time. It focuses on a girl, her 2 siblings, and a strange amulet.

Untitled Project

Untitled Project is a shrot story that takes place right smack in the middle of Project Pearl. During Pearl Rose's disappearance. It focuses on the promenent side-character, Sarah Hazel Mitchell.

VBStroy (Unfinished)

VBStory is a little story adventure made completely in Visual Basic Script. It in any windows version down to Windows XP. I aim to make this compatable with even the Windows 9x systems. (The game's story and humor is inspired off of Sheeptester's The House and The Hotel but borrows elements from Storybook and shares the same universe.)

VisualNovel.nds (Unfinished.)

This visual novel for the Nintendo DSi systems focuses on the universe of Storybook and places you right in it. Very much a fever dream project.
